About Us

The ACM Student Chapter at XIM is a community of passionate individuals aiming to foster technological innovation, promote coding culture, and create valuable learning experiences. Join us as we build skills, collaborate, and push the boundaries of technology.

Mission & Vision

Mission: To bring together like-minded individuals to learn, collaborate, and innovate. We aim to develop and promote a culture of coding, technology, and problem-solving among students.

Vision: To establish a dynamic, technology-driven community at XIM, fostering a collaborative environment that shapes the future of tech and innovation.
